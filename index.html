Bivariate Map

Interactive map showing U.S. natural gas and petroleum power plants as circle markers whose area is based on the total capacity of the power plant. Javascript used to dynamically create popups including plant name, total capacity, and capacity by type, including natural gas, petroleum, and any other sources of energy produced.

Made with Leaflet with data from the U.S. Energy Information Administration.
